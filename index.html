Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is the first stage of this Pokemon's evolution. Bulbasaur debuted in Pokemon Red and Blue as a Starter Pokemon. Known as the Seed Pokemon, Bulbasaur can survive for days solely on sunlight.

Ivysaur

Bulbasaur evolves into Ivysaur at level 16. Ivysaur is a dual type Grass/Poison type Pokemon. Its blossom on its back grows larger in the sunlight. As the blossom grows, Ivysaur loses the ability to stand on its hind legs.

Venusaur

Venusaur is a combination of the words Venus and the Greek saur (lizard). It can Mega Involve into Mega Venasaur using the Venusaurite. This Pokemon is rarely found in the wild, but has been known to inhabit grasslands.
